Map showing car crime rates in Malmo 2016

Purpose: To highlight the areas with the highest and lowest prevalence of car theft:
The map shows the occurance of car crimes in Malmo in the different areas of Malmo. The areas of Central Malmo showed the highest car crime rate. Parts extending to the Northern parts of Malmo, North-western (harbour) and parts of North eastern also had quite high car crime rates. However, other parts of Malmo showed very few car crime rate. The high car crime rate in the central region and other parts could be attributed to the high daily larger population in these areas i.e areas around central region.

Inspiration

This was inspired by the high cases of violence, crimes and gun shootings in Malmo over the years. I had access to Malmo data and I thought it was reasonable to look into the various car theft in Malmo.

Process

The vector data of airports, hydro-regions, railroads and roads were added to Arcgis Pro. The 3 hydro-zones of interests were selected by location and the data were saved in a shapefile. The same process was carried out for dams and airports within the hydro zones of interests.
