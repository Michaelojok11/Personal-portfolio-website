Site Suitability analysis for hazardous site in Puerto Rico

Purpose: Purpose of the site suitability analysis for the federal government of Puerto Rico to locate a hazardous material facility with the following criteria:
- The sites must be only in these three hydrological zones:Interior Puerto Rico, Cibuco Guajataca or Culebrinas-Guanajibo.
- Must be within 2 miles of roads that has a road type (RDLNTYPE) of 2, OR must be within 5 miles of railroads.
- Must NOT be within 5 miles of airports.
- Must NOT be within 10 miles of dams.

Inspiration

This was part of a personal research project to explore the different ways of using data and also the fact that the people in Puerto Rico dispose of about 5.56 pounds of waste per person per day which is more than the national average in the United States of America.

Process

Data in the vector analysis - Airports, dams, Hydro-regions, municipalities, Railroads, roads. The sites should be only in the three hydrological zones, The areas of interests which are the 3 hydrological sites were selected using the select by attributes tool. Dams within the area of interests • Merge data management tools. • Roads and rail in the area of interests were then merged. • 2 miles buffer zones were created around the merged roads and rail • 5 miles buffer created around the airports • 10 miles buffer created around the dams. - Then we create a union airport buffer and dams buffer using the union analysis tool to create a union. - Upon creating this, we can therefore use the erase analysis tool. Combines the two layers and keeps the areas that are not overlapping. These areas are considered the hazardous areas which are not suitable for our analysis. - Remember also that the areas should also be within the three select hydrological zones. - A clip tool was therefore used to keep the areas which are within the 3 hydrological zones (By clipping the hazardous sites and the areas of interests i.e the 3 hydrological zones) - Using the measure distance tool to verify the distances and if we have met all the criteria
