Reference Map of Skane region

The map above shows the geographic location and the boundaries of the different municipalities that combines to form the Skane region. It also display the major roads in the region, the water body that borders parts of the region. The Map also displays the different train stations and the railroads in the region. The map shows that there are more train stations closer to the western parts of Skane compared to other regions and the roads in this western parts are also closer to the rail roads compared to other regions.
The reference map was created using QGIS.
